Leadership Forums

Places to ask questions or discuss leadership topics with others.

Slack Workspaces

  • Engineering Managers - “a safe, confidential space for engineering managers new to the role to chat and support each other.”

  • Rands Leadership Slack - Started by Michael Lopp (aka Rands). See the Welcome post for instructions on getting an invite.

  • Software Lead Weekly Slack - Started by Oren Ellenbogen as an offshoot of his Software Lead Weekly mailing list. Instructions on getting an invite come in the newsletter.

Web Forums

  • Ask a Manager - Discussions on a variety of management topics, each triggered by a question from a reader.

  • Manager Tools - A portion of the Manager Tools site that allows for forum discussions.

  • Reddit CS Career Questions - Many questions revolve around direct development career options, but some involve management.
