Test-Driven Development

Test-driven development with mutation testing, 2020 > It presents an old idea of mutation testing in a new light of extending traditional test-driven development with it. It includes results of an experiment which was conducted to check whether TDD+M is better in terms of test efficiency than traditional TDD.

In industrial teams

Realizing quality improvement through test driven development: results and experiences of four industrial teams. This paper is important because it one of the few instances of quantitative research about TDD in industrial teams (not in controlled environments)
